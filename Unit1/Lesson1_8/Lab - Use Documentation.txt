What are three of the primary responsibilities of a UIView object?

  1. The object views draw content in their rectangular area using UIKit or Core Graphics.
  2. The object views may contain zero or more subviews
  3. A object view is a subclass of UIResponder and can respond to touches and other types of events. 

What does documentation call a view that's embedded in another view?

  -Subview

What does documentation call the parent view that's embedding the other view?

  -Superview

What is a view's frame?

  -The origin and dimensions of the view in the coordinate system of its superview.

How is a view's bounds different from its frame?

  -It defines the internal dimensions of the view as it sees them. It's use is almost exclusive to custom drawing code.
